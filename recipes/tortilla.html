Tortilla Recipe

What is this recipe?

Fry an egg, melt the cheese, and warm your tortilla all in the same pan.

Add your favorite toppings–like salsa, jalapeño pepper slices, cilantro, and green onions–and enjoy a great tasting breakfast, done in a flash. Of course, you can repeat, as many times as you like, if you have company.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 teaspoon taco seasoning, or to taste
  • 1/2 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 (6 to 7-inch) flour tortilla
